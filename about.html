About Us

Who We Are

Founded in London (2003) with African roots, ZOPO MAN brings British tailoring excellence to Nairobi's fashion scene. We specialize in premium men's wear that combines sophistication with urban edge.

What We Do

We craft timeless suits, shirts, and accessories for the modern gentleman. With 30+ stores across Africa, we blend traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design to redefine men's fashion.

Why Choose Us

From our breathable suit fabrics to handcrafted leather goods, every ZOPO MAN product reflects our commitment to quality, comfort, and exceptional style at accessible prices.
